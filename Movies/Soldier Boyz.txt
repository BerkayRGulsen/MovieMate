soldier boy (russian: , romanized: soldatik) is a 2019 russian-language film. it is based on the real-life story of youngest warrior of world war ii, sergei aleshkov.


== synopsis ==
the film is based on true story of a six-year-old boy who became a russian war hero during world war ii. the film shows the struggle of war and the horrific scene the boy of such a age witnessed during the war time. seryozha aleshkov lost his family, was rescued by an army regiment, and engaged in battles.
