the woman condemned is a 1934 american pre-code film directed by dorothy davenport, best known as a silent actress and wife of wallace reid.


== premise ==
a radio star takes a vacation and is later found murdered. barbara (dell) is accused of murder, while jerry (hemingway) tries to prove her innocent.
