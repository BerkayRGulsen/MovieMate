tekken () is a 1990 japanese film directed by junji sakamoto. it stars takeshi yamato, bunta sugawara and karen kirishima.


== plot ==
the plot centers around a young ex-prisoner who takes up boxing (tekken is japanese for "clenched fist").


== awards ==
bunta sugawara won a hochi film award for best actor for his performance.


== other uses ==
this movie has no relation to the tekken video game series or the video game based 2010 film counterpart, even using the same name. another version was released in 2009.
