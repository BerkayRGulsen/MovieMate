my lottery dream home is an american reality television series on hgtv. it is hosted by david bromstad. it premiered on march 7, 2015. it is produced by 7beyond, a joint venture between beyond international and seven network.on october 19, 2020, it was announced that a spin-off titled my lottery dream home international will premiere in 2021.


== premise ==
the show follows david bromstad as he helps lottery winners find a luxurious property after striking it rich.


== production ==
season 1 premiered on march 7, 2015. season 2 premiered on january 6, 2017. season 3 premiered on june 2, 2017. season 4 premiered on february 9, 2018. season 5 premiered on september 21, 2018. season 6 premiered on january 1, 2019. season 7 premiered on april 5, 2019. season 8 premiered on december 13, 2019.
