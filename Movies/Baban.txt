baban is a 2018 indian marathi-language comedy-drama film directed by bhaurao karhade


== plot ==
in an endeavor to convert the traditional business into a big one, ambitious baban strives hard to fulfill his dreams of prosperity. but the circumstances lead him elsewhere, which in-turn gives rise to a storm.

== reception ==
baban received criticism from critics, ganesh matkari of pune mirror called "the narrative keeps hitting and missing alternately throughout the duration of baban, finally concluding on an unsatisfactory note". according to lokmat "baban does not satisfies the audience expectations"the film collected 3.25 crore (us$460,000) in its opening weekend and 8.5 crore (us$1.2 million) in 10 days. the film collected around 15 crore (us$2.1 million) in its 50-day run.
