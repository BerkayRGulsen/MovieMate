anukokunda oka roju (transl.unexpectedly, one day) is a 2005 indian telugu-language neo-noir mystery-thriller film written and directed by chandra sekhar yeleti and produced by gangaraju gunnam and venkat dega. the film stars jagapati babu, charmme kaur and shashank with the music composed by m.m. keeravani.the film received two nandi awards (including second best feature film), a filmfare award south, and santosham film award. it was remade as sunday (2008) in hindi language.


== plot ==
sahasra is a chorus singer struggling to make a career in playback singing. one night she attends a rave at the behest of her friend. the next day, life seems as usual for sahasra. however, she is oblivious to one small detail: the next day is actually the day after. she is also completely unaware of what happened during that one missing day. she realises that something is amiss only when, a few days later, she is chased by unidentified men who try to assassinate her. meanwhile she meets a cab driver(rajesh) who tells her she should give him money back and she hears something strange from her audio recorder which she believes she never said, she also gets weird dreams and gets attacked at theatre, near clinic, near a shop, and in rajesh's house itself. they also find a strange symbol.the rest of the story is about finding the missing links and piecing together the jigsaw puzzle with the help of suresh reddy and rajesh and how love blossoms between rajesh and sahasra.
