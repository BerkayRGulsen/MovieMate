blockbuster is a 2018 french comedy film about a man trying to win back his superhero-loving girlfriend after she dumps him over a video he made to amuse his ailing dad. the film was released worldwide on 24 january 2018 by netflix.


== cast ==

charlotte gabris as lola
tom hygreck as mathias
gunther love as mickael (as sylvain quimne)
laura boujenah as sarah
fod amara as sam
lionel abelanski as simon


== production ==
the film was originally getting off the ground thanks to the crowdfunding platform kisskissbankbank before netflix stepped in.
