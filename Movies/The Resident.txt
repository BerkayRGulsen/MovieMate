the president (german: der prsident) is a 1928 german silent drama film directed by gennaro righelli and starring ivan mozzhukhin, nikolai malikoff and suzy vernon.the film's sets were designed by robert neppach.


== cast ==
ivan mozzhukhin as chico / pepe torre, ein bauer
nikolai malikoff as conde de valdez
suzy vernon as donna manuela, seine tochter
luigi serventi as don germo / geronimo cortez
heinrich schroth as deon ramirez, ein politiker
iwa wanja as jenetz / juez, ein bauernmdchen
rolf as the dog mingo
