don't say a word is sonata arctica's fourth ep released on 30 august 2004 through the label nuclear blast.don't say a word was covered by german symphonic metal band xandria in their 2015 ep fire & ashes.


== track listing ==
all tracks are written by tony kakko.


== personnel ==
tony kakko  vocals, keyboards
jani liimatainen  guitar
marko paasikoski  bass guitar
henrik klingenberg  keyboards, hammond organ
tommy portimo  drums
