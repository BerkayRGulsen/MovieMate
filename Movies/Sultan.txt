sultan is a 2008 indian malayalam-language film directed by sreeprakash, starring vinu mohan and varada jishin in the lead roles.


== plot ==
sivan (vinu mohan) is a medical college student better known in the campus as "sultan" as he was considered by all as the "sultan of romance". he and his four childhood close friends  naufal (anoop chandran) ,sunny (praveenkrishna) and vivek (sreejithravi) form a gang in the campus and they become quite popular among the students.
sivan is in love with nishitha (varada), who is also a student in the same college. their love has been accepted by their families and they have been engaged to marry after the completion of their studies. things take a turn when sivan's pet-name sultan puts him in some totally unexpected situations.
