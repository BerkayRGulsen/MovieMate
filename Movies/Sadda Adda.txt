sadda adda (transl.our spot) is a 2012 indian comedy film directed by muazzam beg. the film was muazzam beg's directorial debut. sadda adda was produced by rajeev agarwal, ramesh agarwal and tarun agarwal, under the banner of rajtaru studios limited.


== plot ==
the film depicts how six bachelors live together, despite their completely different backdrops and personalities like bihari safal yadav manpaur (madhubani). their home, which they call "sadda adda", is an absolute mess of beer bottles, dirty clothes, and mattresses, yet together, they carry out the tasks of cooking, cleaning and paying the rent, while sharing laughter and tears, and always standing up for each other like a family. "sadda adda" starts as a midway room to assist them while they work towards their dreams, but turns out to become their true home.

== production ==
the trailer was released with movie rockstar in theatres. the producers promoted the film with a rock concert at bhavans college in andheri, mumbai. big digital division of reliance broadcasting network ltd has acquired the official music rights.
