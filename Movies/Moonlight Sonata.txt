the moonlight sonata (finnish: kuutamosonaatti) is a 1988 finnish horror comedy film directed by olli soinio.


== premise ==
a female fashion model anni stark (tiina bjrkman) takes leave from the fashion business and goes to lapland for a vacation. little does she know that there's a totally lunatic bunch of local hillbillies living in a nearby farmhouse. the plot thickens as one of the residents begins to harass anni, who is left alone in the wilderness with only her dog to protect her.
