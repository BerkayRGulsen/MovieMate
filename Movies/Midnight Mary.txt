for the song by joey powers, see list of 1960s one-hit wonders in the united states.midnight mary is a 1933 american pre-code crime drama film directed by william a. wellman and starring  loretta young, ricardo cortez, and franchot tone.


== plot ==
the story begins with an indifferent mary martin (young) sitting in a courtroom, on trial for murder. as the jury leaves to deliberate her fate, the story flashbacks on mary's hard life as a woman living in a large city of the 1930s, as well as on the two lusty mena gangster, leo darcy (cortez), and a lawyer, tom mannering, jr. (tone)with whom she is involved.
