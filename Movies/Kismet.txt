kismet is a 1980 indian hindi-language film directed by bhisham kohli, starring mithun chakraborty, ranjeeta, shakti kapoor and om shivpuri.


== plot ==
kismet is a family drama, where mithun and ranjeeta kaur plays the lead role, supported by shakti kapoor and om shivpuri.


== cast ==
mithun chakraborty as moti
ranjeeta as ganga
shakti kapoor as jeevan
om shivpuri as khan
