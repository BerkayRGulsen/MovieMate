for love or money (chinese: ) is a chinese romance film based on hong kong novelist amy cheung's 2006 novel of the same name. the film was directed by gao xixi and starring liu yifei and rain. originally slated for release on 11 november 2014, the film was moved earlier to 7 november 2014.

== box office ==
the film had grossed 65.38 million at the chinese box office.
